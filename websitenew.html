Power Generation

Conventional and Non-Conventional Sources

The company is committed to generating electrical power using a wide range of energy sources, including both conventional and non-conventional methods. Conventional sources such as coal, gas, lignite, and oil are used to meet the increasing demand for power, while non-conventional sources such as solar, wind, biomass, hydel, and tidal waves help in promoting sustainability and reducing dependence on fossil fuels. This diversified energy generation strategy ensures a steady and reliable power supply while contributing to a cleaner environment.

Captive Power Plants

Plants on a Cooperative Basis

The company plays a pivotal role in establishing and managing captive power plants, which are designed to serve a cooperative group of industrial and other consumers. By setting up these plants, the company can provide reliable and cost-effective power directly to participants or through state electricity boards and other authorities. This initiative promotes collaboration among businesses, reduces energy costs, and ensures that companies have access to uninterrupted power, which is crucial for maintaining smooth operations.

Energy Solutions

Comprehensive Supply and Management

R.K.CHAITANYA GREEN ENERGY PVT LTD offers end-to-end solutions for energy generation, accumulation, transmission, distribution, and supply. The company's expertise lies in handling both conventional fuels (such as coal, liquid fuels, LNG, LPG, and coal gas) and non-conventional fuels like biomass. The company undertakes all aspects of power plant management, including owning, erecting, commissioning, operating, and maintaining power plants.

Renewable Energy

Integration and Infrastructure Development

As part of its forward-thinking approach, the company is deeply involved in the construction and operation of renewable energy generation stations. This includes developing infrastructure for solar energy plants, wind farms, and hydel power plants. By integrating these renewable sources into the energy mix, the company is playing a significant role in reducing carbon emissions, supporting green energy initiatives, and contributing to a sustainable future.

Operations & Maintenance

Power Plant Support Services

The company not only focuses on the establishment of power plants but also provides continuous support for their efficient operation and maintenance. This includes monitoring performance, ensuring compliance with safety standards, and performing necessary repairs and upgrades. The maintenance services help to extend the lifespan of power plants and ensure consistent performance, making them reliable for years to come.

Carbon Credits

Trading and Environmental Responsibility

In line with global efforts to mitigate climate change, R.K.CHAITANYA GREEN ENERGY PVT LTD actively participates in carbon credit trading. The company generates renewable energy through non-conventional sources like biomass, which qualifies for carbon credit certification (CERs). These credits can be sold to governments, organizations, or other entities seeking to offset their carbon emissions.
